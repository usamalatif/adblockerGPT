## Design Guidelines

### Colors
- Primary Green: #10a37f (ChatGPT green)
- Dark Background: #202123 (ChatGPT dark)
- Secondary Dark: #343541
- White Text: #FFFFFF
- Gray Text: #9CA3AF

### Typography
- Headlines: Inter Bold/Semibold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Accent: Inter Medium

### Logo
- Use the shield/checkmark icon from cleangpt/icons/
- Green gradient background with white shield
